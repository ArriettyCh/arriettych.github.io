Get Started

What is this website built for?我为什么要创建这个网站？

在大学爬行了一年之后，我发现虽然不乏各种资源，但是它们分散在各处，难以查找。因此，我决定创建这个网站，将自己的课程资料、实习科研经历整合在一起，方便自己查阅，也希望能帮助到你。
After navigating through my first year of university, I realized that while a wealth of resources is available, they are scattered across various platforms, making them difficult to locate. Thus, I resolved to create this website as a centralized repository for my course materials, internship experiences, and research endeavors, not only for my own ease of access but also with the hope of aiding others in their academic journey.

金融学课程 / Finance

 金融前辈的笔记大多是手写或者Word，阅读体验很难说得上好。 但好在金融学的课程通常比较水，看课本或PPT也可以解决绝大多数问题。因此我只记了难度相对较大的课程的笔记。
